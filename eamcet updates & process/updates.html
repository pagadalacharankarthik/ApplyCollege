Counseling Process

1

Registration

Visit the official EAMCET counseling website and register using your hall ticket number and other required details.

2

Payment of Processing Fee

Pay the processing fee through the available payment methods. See below for UPI/QR code payment instructions.

3

Certificate Verification

Attend the offline or online certificate verification at your designated help center with all original documents.

4

Option Entry

Login with hallticket and DOB and enter your college and course preferences in order of priority.

5

Seat Allotment

Check the allotment results and download the allotment order if you're satisfied with the seat.

6

Reporting to College

Report to the allotted college within the specified time frame with all required documents and fee payment.
