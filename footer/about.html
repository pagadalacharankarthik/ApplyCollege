Our Story

Welcome to ApplyCollege! Founded in 2025, we started with a simple mission: to empower students by predicting their ranks and best-fit colleges based on their academic scores, ensuring they secure admissions seamlessly. What began as a small student-centric project has grown into a groundbreaking platform that transforms college admissions into a stress-free, data-driven journey, thanks to the trust and support of our amazing customers.
